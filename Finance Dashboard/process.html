How I made it and what does it do?

I made this financial dashboard for an assignment from my teacher. It was quite tough and took 2-3 weeks. Essentially, I made it using ideas I know (Forms mainly with a bit of simple sections). The CSS was easy too but the JS was quite tough. The form parts were mostly standard, some trims and floats and error avoiders, simple stuff. The local storage came with no struggle either. The tough part was easily the chart.js. I did my fair research on it and tried to make it work and got quite close but succumbed to asking chatGPT to fix that part which went great! Had a lot of fun making this and even more fun doing some extra tweaks(I love doing bonus tweaks to make a page a fun and less robotic site :) ).
